About Us


The Sterling Stage Kampitheater is a venue like no other. Beautiful evergreens create a cozy campground and backdrop for the incredible music that takes place on 2 unique stages. A great place to enjoy with old friends and a fun place to meet new ones. Grills and coolers are permitted, dogs require a ticketed pass (no glass, or fireworks please!!!) and all tickets (including day passes) include overnight camping. The Sterling Stage is nestled in picturesque upstate NY near Lake Ontario and other great tourist stops.

Sterling Stage Kampitheater has been hosting music festivals since 1995.

Notable artists that have played the venue include Rock n' Roll Hall of Fame Inductees... Dickey Betts, Levon Helm, Garth Hudson, and Donna Jean Godchaux. Plus... Commander Cody, New Riders of the Purple Sage w/John "Marmaduke" Dawson, Merl Saunders, Jonathan Edwards, Anders Osborne, John Kadlecik, Max Creek, Ryan Montbleau, John Browns Body, Jazz Mandolin Project, Zach Deputy, Rubblebucket, Giant Panda Guerilla Dub Squad, Hot Buttered Rum, Jackie Greene, Nahko & Medicine For The People, Sister Sparrow & The Dirty Birds, Akron/Family, and Fishbone, plus hundreds of local, regional, and other touring groups too numerous to list.
