From Weedsport and all points west

  • Take I-90 EAST (Thruway) to exit 40
  • Take Rte 34 north to Rte 104
  • Turn right onto Rte 104 east and proceed until you reach Blythe Rd
  • Turn left on Blythe Rd and proceed to Kent Rd.
  • Turn right on Kent Rd. and the gate is on the right 1/4 mile.

From Syracuse and all points south

  • Take Rte 81 north to exit 29 (Rte 481 north)
  • Take Rte 481 north to Fulton
  • Turn left on Rte 3 west towards Hannibal.
  • Turn right onto Rte 104 east and proceed until you reach Blythe Rd
  • Turn left on Blythe Rd and proceed to Kent Rd.
  • Turn right on Kent Rd. and the gate is on the right 1/4 mile.

From Utica and all points east

  • Take I-90 WEST (Thruway) to exit 34a
  • Take Rte 481 north to Fulton
  • Turn left on Rte 3 west towards Hannibal.
  • Turn right onto Rte 104 east and proceed until you reach Blythe Rd
  • Turn left on Blythe Rd and proceed to Kent Rd.
  • Turn right on Kent Rd. and the gate is on the right 1/4 mile.

From Mexico and all points north

  • Take Rte 81 south to exit 34
  • Turn right onto Rte 104 west and go through Oswego
  • Turn right on Blythe Rd and proceed to Kent Rd.
  • Turn right on Kent Rd. and the gate is on the right 1/4 mile.

Many local roads lack signs and there will be few if any festival signs up. If you are unfamiliar with the area please print yourself a detailed map showing local roads and stick to our version for the least amount of confusion. If you get lost and need some help give us a call at 818-212-9489.
